VECNA

Henry Creel, also known as Vecna or One (001), is the main antagonist of Stranger Things Season - 4, portrayed by Jamie Campbell Bower. Once a gifted child with telepathic powers, he killed his family and was later experimented on by Dr. Brenner. After being defeated by Eleven, he was banished to the Upside Down, where he transformed into Vecna. As Vecna, he became the ruler of the Upside Down, controlling its monsters and driving the horrors that haunt Hawkins.
